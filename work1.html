Bali

Bali is an Indonesian island.
Bali is Indonesia's main tourist destination, with a significant rise in tourism since the 1980s. Tourism-related business makes up 80% of its economy.
Bali has beautiful views,beautiful temples and an exciting culture . Its natural beauty has made it a very popular tourist attraction..

The most beautiful places:

  • Ubud
  • Seminyak
  • Uluwatu Temple
